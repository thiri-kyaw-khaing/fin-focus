F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How does FINFOCUS help with budgeting?

FINFOCUS lets you create custom budgets based on your financial goals and track your spending to stay within your budget each month.

Does FINFOCUS track investments like stocks?

NO. FINFOCUS is a only simple financial management app that helps you track and manage your monthly finances, including expenses, income, budgets, savings, and bills.We can't track stock.

Can I access my FINFOCUS account on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access FINFOCUS on any device, whether it's a smartphone, tablet, or computer, as long as you're logged into your account.

Is it a fee to use FINFOCUS?

Yes.FINFOCUS is free to use!It is a free application with core features available at no cost.

What should I do if I have trouble using the app?

If you encounter any issues, FINFOCUS offers in-app support, FAQs, and a customer service team available to help you with any problems or questions.

How do I reset my password?

If you forget your password, simply click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the prompts to reset your password via email.
